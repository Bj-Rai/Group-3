## 📌 Game Overview
**Brain Trek** is an **adventure + survival + educational game** set in a mysterious forest. Players explore the environment, solve subject-based questions, and survive against dangerous creatures while completing meaningful tasks.

---

## 🎯 Theme
- Adventure 🌄  
- Survival 🐍  
- Education 📚  

---

## 📚 Subject Integration (Cross-Pollination)
This game integrates multiple subjects:
- 🌱 Science – Environment & Ecosystem  
- ➗ Mathematics – Problem-solving  
- 💰 Economics – Resource management  
- 📜 History – Knowledge-based questions  

---

## 💡 Game Concept
The player explores a mysterious forest to find hidden treasures using clues like:
- "Check all the trees"
- "Look near the rocks"

To unlock treasures:
- Players must answer academic questions  
- Face enemies like snakes and crabs  
- Complete real-life inspired tasks  

The game promotes both **learning and survival skills**.

---

## 🎓 Learning Objectives
- Understand environmental conservation 🌍  
- Improve knowledge across subjects 📖  
- Develop problem-solving and critical thinking 🧠  
- Practice decision-making and survival strategies ⚔️  
- Promote helping others and social responsibility 🤝  

---

## ⚙️ How the Game Works
- Player starts with limited lives (e.g., 3 ❤️)  
- Explore forest using clues  
- Find treasure → Answer question  
  - ✅ Correct → Gain gold & progress  
  - ❌ Wrong → Lose one life  
- Enemies attack as levels increase  
- Use weapons to defend  
- Complete side tasks for rewards  

🏁 **Game Ends When:**
- All lives are lost  
- OR all levels are completed
